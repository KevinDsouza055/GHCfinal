The Beginning

Where it all started

Grace Home began in 2021 in a small studio apartment in Bandra, Mumbai. Our founder, Grace, had always believed that fragrance had the power to transform a space — to make a room feel warmer, a moment feel more intentional.

What started as a passion project — pouring candles on a kitchen counter with borrowed equipment — quickly became a calling. The first 20 candles sold out in 48 hours. By the end of the year, Grace Home had found its home in hundreds of homes across India.

Our Philosophy

"We don't just make candles. We make the quiet moments between the loud ones."

Every Grace Home candle is an invitation. To slow down. To breathe. To notice the beauty in ordinary evenings.

The Craft

Made by hand.
Never by machine.

Each Grace Home candle is poured by hand in small batches of no more than 50 units. We use a proprietary blend of coconut and soy wax — cleaner-burning, longer-lasting, and more sustainable than paraffin.

Our fragrance oils are sourced from respected perfume houses in Grasse, France, and certified Indian suppliers. Every scent undergoes a minimum 4-week testing process before it earns a place in our collection.

We use lead-free cotton wicks, recyclable glass vessels, and FSC-certified packaging. Because luxury without responsibility isn't true luxury.

Our Values

What we stand for

Intention

Every decision — from the fragrance house we partner with to the typeface on our label — is made with deliberate intention.

Transparency

We share what goes into our candles and what doesn't. No hidden ingredients, no compromises on safety or quality.

Sustainability

Recyclable packaging, natural wax blends, and ethical sourcing. We believe beautiful products shouldn't cost the earth.
