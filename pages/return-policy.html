Return &
Refund Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2025

At Grace Home Candles, we take immense pride in the quality of our hand-poured candles. Every order is carefully inspected and packed before dispatch. However, if something goes wrong, we want to make it right.

Quick Summary

  • ✓ 7-day return window from date of delivery
  • ✓ Returns accepted for damaged or defective products only
  • ✓ Refund or replacement offered — your choice
  • ✓ Refunds processed within 7–10 business days
  • ✗ No returns for change of mind or fragrance preference

1. Eligibility for Returns

We accept returns under the following circumstances:

  • The product arrived damaged (e.g. broken glass, crushed packaging)
  • The product is defective (e.g. wick fault, significant cosmetic flaw from production)
  • You received the wrong product
  • The product is missing from your order

We do not accept returns for:

  • Change of mind or personal fragrance preference
  • Minor natural variations in colour, texture, or surface finish (common in hand-poured candles)
  • Products that have been lit or used
  • Products returned without prior authorisation
  • Claims made after 7 days of confirmed delivery

2. How to Initiate a Return

To request a return, please follow these steps within 7 days of receiving your order:

  • Step 1: Email us at returns@gracehomecandles.in with your order number and a description of the issue.
  • Step 2: Attach clear photographs of the damaged/defective product and the packaging.
  • Step 3: Our team will review your request within 1–2 business days and confirm whether your return is approved.
  • Step 4: If approved, we will arrange a reverse pickup at no cost to you (for eligible claims). Do not ship the product back without our confirmation.

Returns must be in their original packaging (or securely re-packed to prevent transit damage).

3. Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the refund decision within 2 business days. If approved:

  • Refunds will be credited to your original payment method (UPI, card, net banking, etc.)
  • Processing time: 7–10 business days (bank processing times may vary)
  • Razorpay refunds may take an additional 2–3 days to reflect in your account

Alternatively, you may opt for a replacement of the same product at no additional cost, which is often faster than a refund.

4. Exchanges

We do not offer like-for-like exchanges for fragrance preference. However, if you received a defective product, you may request a replacement of the same product.

5. Damaged in Transit

If your order arrives visibly damaged (damaged outer box), please:

  • Accept the package but note the damage with the delivery person where possible
  • Photograph the outer packaging before opening
  • Photograph the contents upon opening
  • Email us within 24 hours of delivery at returns@gracehomecandles.in

Failure to document and report transit damage within 24 hours may affect your eligibility for a claim.

6. Gift Orders

If a Grace Home product was received as a gift and is damaged or defective, the gift recipient may contact us directly. Please provide the order number (usually included in the packaging) and proof of purchase if possible.
