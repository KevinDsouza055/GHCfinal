Orders & Shipping

How long does delivery take?

Orders are dispatched within 1–2 business days. Standard delivery across India takes 3–5 business days. Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad) may receive orders in 2–3 days.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! All orders above ₹999 qualify for free shipping across India. Orders below ₹999 attract a flat shipping fee of ₹99.

Can I track my order?

Yes. Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via email and WhatsApp (if provided). You can use this to monitor your delivery in real time.

Do you ship outside India?

Currently we ship only within India. International shipping is something we're actively working towards — sign up to our newsletter to be notified when it's available.

Our Products

What wax do you use?

We use a proprietary blend of coconut wax and soy wax. This blend burns cleaner than paraffin, has a longer burn time, and provides excellent fragrance throw. It is also biodegradable and sustainably sourced.

Are your candles safe for pets and children?

Our candles use lead-free cotton wicks and phthalate-free fragrance oils. However, we always recommend burning candles out of reach of children and pets, in ventilated spaces, and never leaving a burning candle unattended.

How do I get the best burn from my candle?

For the first burn, allow the wax to melt to the full edge of the vessel (approximately 2–3 hours). This prevents tunnelling. Trim your wick to 5mm before each subsequent burn. Never burn for more than 4 hours at a time. Keep away from draughts and flammable surfaces.

How long do your candles burn?

Our 300g candles burn for 60–80 hours depending on the fragrance and environmental conditions. Following our candle care instructions will help you achieve the full burn time.

Returns & Payments

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for damaged or defective products. Due to the nature of our products, we cannot accept returns for change of mind. Please see our full Return Policy for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods via Razorpay — UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit & debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and EMI options on select cards. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

My payment failed. What should I do?

If your payment failed, please check with your bank first. If the amount was debited but you didn't receive a confirmation, please contact us at hello@gracehomecandles.in with your order details and we'll resolve it within 24 hours.

Gifting & Corporate

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Every Grace Home order arrives in our signature packaging — archival tissue paper, ribbon, and our rigid gift box. A personalised gift message can be added at checkout at no extra charge.

Do you do bulk / corporate gifting orders?

Yes, we love corporate gifting! For orders of 10 or more candles, we offer custom packaging, branded labels, and special pricing. Email us at gifting@gracehomecandles.in or visit our Gifting page to learn more.
